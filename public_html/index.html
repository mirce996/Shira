SHIRA

Shira Choir is composed of true musical connoisseurs. Bound to the
beat by the baton of Shraga Gold, its talented voices harmoniously
entertain musically astute crowds worldwide. They are known to
flawlessly recreate the most intricate melodies of time-honored
classics made famous by the likes of Avraham Fried, Mordechai Ben
David, Suki and Ding Productions, Lipa Shmeltzer, Yaakov Shwekey
and other Jewish music giants.
