Services

Song. And style.

It’s your special event – not open mic night. So you need vocalists
capable of setting a specific mood, carefully crafting an appropriate
song list, and stylizing the music to reflect the nuances of your
celebration. That’s why you choose Shira Choir. You book talent
backed by experience. Shira has truly done it all, to the tune of
4,000 performances over the past 11 years:

  • Lead
  • Backup
  • Original Score
  • Accapella
  • Entertainment for Children
  • Children’s Choir
  • Hachnosas Sefer Torah
  • Vocal Coaching

Want Shira front and center? Make one call and your search for singers is done. Shira works closely with the band you booked and serves up all the vocal performances your event can possibly want. From weddings to bar/bat mitzvahs, from seudas bris to seudas hodaah, from fundraising events to business conferences – Shira works every stage.

No concert is complete without Shira’s masterful harmonies enhancing and embellishing the performances of your lead singers. Shira arranges its vocal talents according to the lead’s preferences and the conductor’s directive. For any event worthy of a top lead vocalist, Shira is the #1 backup choir of choice for the discerning listener.

Whether you’re putting together a slide presentation or are seeking the perfect tonal backdrop for your music album, Shira Choir offers original score production and live choir recording to suit every taste, tempo and timeframe. You’ll have your audience’s ear, as they’ll listen, connect, and remember your message long after they’ve heard it.

 When full-blown music is inappropriate or not allowed – such as in the days of sefira, the three weeks, or in the presence of an individual who’s in aveilus – Shira Choir ensures that your event won’t be lacking for joy or celebratory feel. Shira’s impeccable accpella arrangements bring life to your event with their renowned musicality and timing.

Shira Choir makes music that imbues these little hearts with the joy of youth and life. From kid-friendly concerts at camp to age-specific shows at school, an afternoon of entertainment by Shira Choir gets little hands clapping to the beat. Give them a truly enjoyable experience that will remain with them for many years to come.

Shira’s children’s choir is available for booking for albums, private events and other occasions. Under the tutelage of Shraga Gold, Shira’s founder and music director, Shira junior produces pure, soulful music delivered by the most talented boys ages 8 to 13. At request, Shira can provide any combination of adult/child performers.

Pomp and perfection: Shira arranges for a magnificent truck and sound system to accompany the choir. A dazzling chuppah serves as the procession’s focal point, and signs, torches, flags, and pecklach are available for the children in attendance. From k’sivas osios to the seudas mitzvah, Shira creates a beautiful, uplifting and inspiring event.

The exceptionally talented members of Shira Choir each have years of musical training and experience behind their incredible voices. Looking to perform at a family event? Want to daven before the amud? Getting ready to launch your own music career? Have Shira provide you with personal vocal coaching so that your singing soars.
